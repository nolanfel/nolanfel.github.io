Michigan Fishing

PROBLEM

Funds needed to support the University of Michigan Fishing community.

IDEA GENERATION

Create a merchandising plan that will raise funds, increase brand awareness, and provide community members with custom gear.

IDEA SCREENING

The market for raising funds on campus is over-saturated.

Fishing is a niche sport that would not receive significant traffic if competing with other student organizations.

MARKETING STRATEGY

Created a solid, distinct brand.

Target audience is the University of Michigan Fishing Community.

Used a website and social platforms for digital promotion of products.

Used representation at fishing-related conventions and word of mouth for physical promotion of products.

Previous Next
BUSINESS ANALYSIS

Low cost of production

Funds are currently stagnant as there are not enough funds to significantly support fishing team

Revenue from complete sale of a single order of shirts would nearly triple funds

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

Worked with local printing company to develop finalized designs according to order demands.

RESULT

Tested merchandising plan with the sale of a single order of shirts

Sold out within two weeks

Nearly tripled revenue, as predicted
