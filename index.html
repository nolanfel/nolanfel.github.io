Orb

Project Background

Multitasking is now easier with Orb. Simply record thoughts and ideas that are important into the extension. While you browse, Orb wil notify you if and when something related to one of your thoughts appears on a page! This project is still in progress.

Project Responsibilities

Coordinated the build of a chrome extension which enhances the browsing experience by providing a proactive thought tracker.

Constructed a marketing website promoting the chrome extension providing a medium which illustrates the product's pragmatic features.

Defined and designed Orb's brand resulting in easily identifiable logos and content.

Troy Civic Center

Project Background

The green space in Troy, MI was being threatened by pressure from private interests seeking to unreasonably expand the city's downtown district. The Troy Civic Center is a serene plot of parkland where thousands of citizens come together and enjoy recreational activities on a weekly basis. The movement was not successful in vote, but the close vote raised significant awareness for the cause and resulted in the wavering of private interests and the current security of the parkland.

Project Responsibilities

Collaborated with local politicians to create a website promoting a cmpaign and providing an online platform for supporters to mobilize and communicate with movement leaders.
